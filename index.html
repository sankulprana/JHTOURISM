Popular Destinations

From serene hills and majestic waterfalls to sacred temples and lush national parks, Jharkhand has it all.

Netarhat

The 'Queen of Chotanagpur', famous for its breathtaking sunrises and sunsets.

Patratu Valley

A stunning landscape with winding roads and a massive dam.

Deoghar

A major Hindu pilgrimage site, home to the Baidyanath Temple.

Betla National Park

A diverse wildlife sanctuary with tigers, elephants, and rich flora.

Hundru Falls

Witness the Subarnarekha river fall from a magnificent height of 322 feet.
